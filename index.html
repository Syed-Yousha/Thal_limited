Thal Limited started its journey in 1966. A public listed entity, TL is listed on the Karachi and Lahore Stock Exchanges.

Operating under the umbrella of the prestigious House of Habib Group, Thal Limited derives its strength both from its parent group as well as its values and cherishes its own diversity. The House of Habib does not need any introduction employing over 10,000 people, HOH is an established and mature corporate player providing an array of products to people. From automobiles to audio media, building materials to banking and computers to chemicals, HOH has its imprints across a variety of sectors.
